1. Introduction

Welcome to the Electric Meter Bill Tracker (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your personal data.

This privacy policy applies to all users of our App available on iOS, Android, and Web platforms. By accessing and using this App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy. The App is used to manage electric meter records and service access. Any service billing arranged with your provider is handled outside the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create and use an account in Electric Meter Bill Tracker, we collect information needed to provide the service, such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Account role and service-access status
  • Account creation, update, and last-login metadata
  • Authentication credentials processed by Firebase Authentication

We do not view or store your plain-text password inside the App database. Sign-in is handled through Google Firebase Authentication.

2.2 Meter Information

To help you track your electricity consumption, we collect and store the following meter-related data:

  • Meter name/identifier
  • Meter number (if provided)
  • Meter location (if provided)
  • Default rate per unit (electricity rate)
  • Meter creation and update dates

2.3 Bill Information

When you add billing records to the App, we collect:

  • Bill date and time
  • Previous meter reading
  • Current meter reading
  • Units consumed
  • Rate per unit (for that billing period)
  • Bill amount
  • Amount paid
  • Previous due amount
  • Current due amount
  • Bill creation and update dates

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

To operate the App securely and reliably, limited technical information may be processed automatically, including:

  • Device and App Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and language settings
  • Service Logs: Basic activity records needed to synchronize your meter and bill data across sessions
  • Technical Diagnostics: Limited error and support-related information needed to troubleshoot issues

The App is not designed to collect contacts, precise location, photos, microphone recordings, health data, or payment card information as part of its normal use.

2.5 Information You Provide

You may also choose to provide additional information, including:

  • Support messages, feedback, or suggestions
  • Optional notes related to service activation or support
  • Account deletion requests and any optional reason you include
Note: The App does not collect highly sensitive personal information such as government ID numbers, bank account details, payment card details, biometric data, or precise location. Your name, email, and any support details you voluntarily provide are used only to deliver and support the service.

3.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Service Improvement: To understand operational issues and improve the App's features

  • Personalization: To customize your App experience based on your preferences
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3.3 Non-Usage of Your Data

We explicitly do not:

  • Sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent
  • Share your bill or meter data with utility companies or external organizations
  • Use your information for targeted advertising
  • Combine your data with data from other sources for profiling
  • Process card payments or in-app purchases inside the App

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage Location

Your account information and app data are stored using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Data is protected using industry-standard security measures such as encrypted transmission and controlled access.

4.2 Security Measures

We employ the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS) during transmission
  • Access Control: Only authenticated users can access their own data through security rules
  • Firestore Security Rules: Role-based access control ensures users can only access their own records
  • Secure Authentication: Firebase Authentication with email/password authentication
  • Administrative Controls: Administrative access is restricted to authorized personnel for service support and review tasks
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we commit to protecting your information using reasonable security practices.

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, enforce our terms, and comply with legal obligations. When you submit an account deletion request, we will review and process it within a reasonable time. Once approved, related app data is deleted or anonymized as operationally appropriate, unless retention is legally required.
